Flaming Cliffs: About Us
 

Our Mission

At the Flaming Cliffs Administration, our mission is to preserve, study, and share the rich paleontological heritage of the Flaming Cliffs (Bayanzag). We are dedicated to safeguarding this unique site, promoting scientific research, and providing educational experiences that connect visitors to its historical and natural significance.

 

We envision the Flaming Cliffs as a globally recognized center for paleontological research and eco-tourism, where visitors can immerse themselves in the wonders of Earth's prehistoric past while contributing to the preservation of this invaluable heritage.

Our Objectives

Conservation
Research
Community Engagement
Education

Implement sustainable practices to protect the geological and paleontological integrity of the Flaming Cliffs.

Support and collaborate on scientific studies that enhance our understanding of the site's fossils and geological formations.

Work closely with local communities to promote cultural heritage and create economic opportunities through eco-tourism.

Develop programs and exhibits that inform and inspire the public about the significance of the Flaming Cliffs and its discoveries.

Our Team

 

Our team comprises passionate professionals, including paleontologists, conservationists, educators, and community liaisons, all committed to preserving the legacy of the Flaming Cliffs for future generations.

Join Us

 

We invite you to visit the Flaming Cliffs, participate in our programs, and support our efforts to protect and celebrate this extraordinary window into Earth's distant past.
